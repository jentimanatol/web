Explore the Ocean's Marvels: Giant Ocean Tank: Meet Myrtle the green sea turtle and her friends in the four-story Giant Ocean Tank. Watch as they glide gracefully through the water, surrounded by vibrant coral reefs and a dazzling array of fish. Marine Mammal Center: Get up close and personal with playful northern fur seals and sea lions. Witness their acrobatic antics and learn about their fascinating behaviors from our expert trainers. Touch Tank: Feel the thrill of touching sharks and rays at the interactive Touch Tank. It's a hands-on experience that will leave you in awe of these incredible creatures. Exciting Exhibits and Activities: Penguin Colony: Say hello to our charming African penguins as they waddle and swim in their specially designed habitat. Their playful personalities are sure to bring a smile to your face. Whale Watch: Join us for an exhilarating Whale Watch cruise in partnership with Boston Harbor City Cruises. Spot majestic whales breaching the surface and learn about their conservation from our knowledgeable guides. Simons Theatre: Immerse yourself in the wonders of the ocean with 3D nature documentaries on the largest movie screen in New England. It's an experience you won't want to miss. Conservation and Education: Anderson Cabot Center for Ocean Life: Discover the groundbreaking research and conservation efforts led by our dedicated scientists. Learn how we're working to protect marine life and preserve our blue planet for future generations. Daily Presentations: Enjoy informative presentations and feedings at various exhibits, including the Marine Mammal Center and the Harbor Seal Exhibit. Our experts are always ready to share their knowledge and answer your questions.
