Welcome to Aquariums to be Seen — your premier guide to discovering the mesmerizing underwater worlds within New England and beyond. We are passionate storytellers, ocean lovers, and curious minds dedicated to promoting the beauty, science, and wonder of aquariums everywhere. Whether you're a lifelong marine enthusiast or a curious first-time visitor, our mission is to connect you with the most captivating aquatic experiences this region—and the world—has to offer. From the bustling shores of Boston to the tranquil coastlines of Maine, we shine a spotlight on every public aquarium that educates, conserves, and inspires. We believe aquariums are more than just attractions — they are living classrooms where conservation meets curiosity, and where people of all ages can develop a deeper understanding of life beneath the waves. We proudly feature direct links to iconic marine institutions like the New England Aquarium, Mystic Aquarium, The Maritime Aquarium, Seacoast Science Center, and the Maine State Aquarium. Our platform is built to inform, engage, and help you plan unforgettable aquatic adventures, all while supporting the ongoing efforts of marine preservation. So dive in. Explore. Learn. And join us as we celebrate the incredible aquatic life that swims just beneath the surface—and the passionate people working to protect it.
